Personal Coaching

You often find yourself feeling lost, your mind is overwhelmed, stress is knocking you out of your shoes and you continuously worry about past or the future? Does that sound any familiar? Do not worry. TAKE A DEEP BREATH. You are not the first and most surely - not the only one! Let us help you bring effective and sustainable change in your personal life. Together, we can shape a successful future plan for your development and success!

Workshops

Our team believes that knowledge through practice is the most sufficient way, besides that sense of the community, self- realization, comfortable and joyful environment makes a huge difference. Are you looking for a self-development retreat or workshop week? Academy of Cognizance are working on different concepts accordingly to the need of our community.

Corporate Coaching

Visualize the life factors which are holding your back, and recognize how your approach to work translates into results. During our coaching sessions, you’ll take a full audit of your professional experience, and identify opportunities for healthy growth. Need a deeper understanding of how to turn self - improvement into business success? Academy of Cognizance Team accordingly to your company’s needs, will create a training program that will focus on the aims and goals of the individual and company needs.

Retreats

Make sure that your team's spirit is full of creativity and desire to perform even more than before. Create some time and space for each one of the individuals to express and enjoy themselves. Academy of Cognizance understood how important Teambuilding practice is in order to keep the fire burning. Personalized, fun, and educative experience is ALREADY HERE FOR YOU AND YOUR TEAM! Provide yourself and your team with the best experiences and open environment.
